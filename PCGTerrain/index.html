Move with the arrow keys, click to change terrain, enter a world seed for a new world.

This generator creates natural terrain using Perlin noise and a seed created with the xxHash hashing algorithm. Perlin noise is also used to scatter different plants and details, as well as the subtle movement of the water. I wanted to create Minecraft-like terrain and experimented with laying down different blocks like sand depending on their height/closeness to water. Overall, it looks as I intended it to be and I should I continue developing it, I want to add more complex generation like biomes and structures.
